# SentryMate

> **Never miss what just happened.**
> A dashcam for your Mac.

SentryMate is a beautifully simple macOS menu bar app that continuously records your screen in the background — so when something important happens, you can save it **after** it happens.

Whether you're capturing a bug, preserving an important meeting moment, or saving an unexpected workflow, SentryMate makes sure you're always covered.

---

## Why SentryMate?

Because sometimes the most important moments are the ones you didn’t know you needed to record.

* **Catch bugs after they happen**
  No need to reproduce an issue from memory.

* **Save meeting highlights instantly**
  Recover key moments without recording the entire call manually.

* **Record your workflow automatically**
  Perfect for demos, tutorials, or reviewing your process.

---

## How It Works

SentryMate continuously records your Mac in the background while keeping only the most recent footage.

Old recordings are automatically discarded, maintaining a rolling buffer of up to:

> **1 hour 30 minutes**

When you need it, click the menu bar icon and export the recent recording instantly.

## Built With Privacy in Mind

You stay in control of what gets recorded.

* Record **screen only**
* Include **system audio**
* Include **microphone**
* Choose specific **displays**
* Keep everything **stored locally on your Mac**

No cloud uploads. No remote storage. No surprises.
